The Squad Truck Team's primary mission is to rescue the entrapped victims of motor vehicle accidents. The team is available for other heavy-duty rescues, mass casualty incidents, lighting, and dive and air bottle refilling. All Squad Truck Team members are also active ALS or BLS providers.
