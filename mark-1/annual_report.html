Administrative Standards and Practices

    The Kempsville Volunteer Rescue Squad:

    • Meets the Better Business Bureau's Standards for Charitable Solicitations. A copy of the BBB report is available upon request.
    • Requires that two corporate officers sign each of its checks, and that no expenditure be made without an official written purchase order.
    • Receives financial planning assistance from Raymond James Financial, Inc., and abides by its published Investment Allocation Plan.
    • Is audited annually by Corbin & Company of Chesapeake, Virginia.
    • Is a solicitor of charitable contributions, and holds a Category G (Civic Organization) Exemption from Annual Registration from the Virginia Department of Agriculture and Consumer Services (VDACS), Office of Consumer Affairs, Regulatory Programs Unit, in accordance with the Virginia Solicitation of Contributions Act.

Tax-Exempt Status

    The Kempsville Volunteer Rescue Squad is tax exempt under section 501(C)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code. It is eligible to receive contributions deductible as charitable donations for federal income tax purposes. A copy of the IRS Letter of Determination of 501(C)(3) Status is available upon request.

Definition of Fiscal Year

    The KVRS fiscal year runs from July 1st to June 30th.
