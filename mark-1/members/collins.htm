Evan Baldwin Collins

Evan Baldwin Collins VIRGINIA BEACH - Evan Baldwin Collins, 20, of Coventry Road passed away unexpectedly May 15, 2011. Evan was born in Cherry Point, N.C., October 1990 to Brian and Kymberly Collins. He was currently employed as a dispatcher with Medical Transport and a volunteer with the Kempsville Rescue Squad. Evan loved the outdoors and anything to do with the water, running and weight lifting. He is survived by his parents, Kym and Steve Bach, and Brian and Carol Collins; grandparents, Dave and Jaynie Carlson, Mary Garner, and Steve and Beth Evans-Bach; siblings, Wesley, Mason, Taylor, Victoria, Meghan, Craig and Kaylene; and numerous aunts, uncles and cousins. A service to celebrate his life will be held Friday, May 20, at 2 p.m. in Graham Funeral Home with the Rev. Ken Carlton officiating. The family will receive friends at the funeral home Thursday from 5 to 8 p.m. The family request in lieu of flowers that memorial contributions can be made through Evan's "Book of Memories" at www.grahamfuneralhome. com. For more information or for those unable to attend, you may also watch the webcast and leave a note to the family.
