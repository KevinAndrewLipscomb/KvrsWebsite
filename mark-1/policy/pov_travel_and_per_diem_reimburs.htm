KEMPSVILLE RESCUE SQUAD INC.

POLICY STATEMENT

1998-10-11-01

Date:
11 October 1998
Title:
POV Travel and Per Diem Reimbursement

It shall be the policy of the Kempsville Rescue Squad to reimburse people fairly, and without regard for their personal traveling, lodging, and dining tastes, for expenses incurred as a result of squad-related out-of-town travel.

To this end, the following stipulations are enacted:

  1. The squad elects to make Per Diem reimbursements in accordance with federal Travel Management Policy -- Per Diem Rates available at <http://policyworks.gov/org/main/mt/homepage/mtt/perdiem/travel.shtml>.
  2. The squad elects to make reimbursements for POV travel in accordance with federal Travel Management Policy -- Privately Owned Vehicles (POV) Mileage Reimbursement Rate information available at <http://policyworks.gov/org/main/mt/homepage/mtt/pov.htm>.

APPROVED:

Kevin Lipscomb, President, 11 Oct 1998
