340-KVRS Main Greeting

Thanks for calling the Kempsville Volunteer Rescue Squad.

IF YOU ARE REPORTING AN ILLNESS OR INJURY, HANG UP AND DIAL 9-1-1 NOW.

Otherwise, please select from the following choices:

For information on current media promotions or news items, press "9".

For our mailing address, press "1".

For information on joining the squad, press "2".

For accounts payable, press "3".

For donations, fund drive concerns, requests for demonstrations, or to reach our president, press "4".

For case follow-ups, customer service concerns, nonemergency ambulance transports, special event standbys, or to reach our captain, press "5".

For a general introduction to the squad, press "6".

For internal business, press "7".

To speak to someone during business hours, press "8".
