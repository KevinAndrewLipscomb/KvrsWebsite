STANDING RULE
Time and Place of Regular Business Meetings

Date of original adoption:
Unknown/Traditional
Date of latest revision:
None

Although Bylaws Section 110 specifies the frequency and day of Regular Business Meetings, it does not specify the time of day or the place for such meetings.  Therefore, the time of day of such meetings shall be 7 P.M. and the place of such meetings shall be Virginia Beach Fire & Rescue Station #9.  Furthermore, when such a meeting would coincide with a holiday, such meeting shall be rescheduled for the following Monday.
