STANDARD ADMINISTRATIVE PROCEDURE
Requesting No-Interest Apparatus Loans from City Council

Date of original adoption:
November 1999
Date of latest revision:
None
  1. The first step of the process for securing a no-interest apparatus loan from City Council is to compose and submit a request letter.  Such a letter should state the following:
    • The situation with the vehicle being replaced (eg, maintenance problems, age, whatever);
    • How much the new vehicle costs with or without a trade;
    • How much the corporation intends to pay;
    • How much the corporation wants to get in the loan;
    • How the corporation want to pay back the loan (ie, number of years, amount per year, etc);
  2. When the corporation wants to start the payback (usually one year after securing the loan).
  3. Recommended repayment periods are:
    • Ambulances:  3-5 years
    • Crash trucks:  7-10 years
  4. Address the letter to:

    5. Director
    Department of EMS
    1917 Arctic Avenue
    Virginia Beach, VA 23451

  5. Allow 4-6 weeks for processing by the VBDEMS Director.  Do NOT sign a contract until the loan is approved.  City Council has never denied a recommendation from the VBDEMS Director, but the time element may be an issue.  It takes longer than expected to get the loan through Management Services.

KVRS wishes to thank Bruce Edwards, Director, VBDEMS, for the information included in this S.A.P.
