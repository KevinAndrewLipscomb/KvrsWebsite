Simulation of a Code Red (cardiac arrest resuscitation effort) in the back of an ambulance (April 1997). EMT Shamera Boone is performing chest compressions. EMT Ryan Mclane is providing artificial respirations with a bag-valve-mask device. By coordinating their efforts, Boone and Mclane are performing CPR. Paramedic Dan Moore (left) is making a report on the MED/COR radio to the medical control physician at the hospital. A fourth technician (EMT Judy Paulsen) is partially visible (top right) as she adjusts the IV for proper administration of fluid and medication. The patient is also attached to a cardiac monitor-defibrillator. Even in the field, modern EMS providers can give this patient virtually all of the treatments that he or she would initially receive at the hospital emergency room. The "patient" in this simulation is actually a CPR training manakin.
