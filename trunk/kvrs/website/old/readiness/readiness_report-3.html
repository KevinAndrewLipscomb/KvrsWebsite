Readiness Report:  Condition 3 (Preparedness Alert)

This is the official KVRS Readiness Report.  Before and during potential states of emergency, check this message frequently for updates.

  • Virginia Beach is currently under a Hurricane Warning.
  • The Department of EMS has issued a Preparedness Alert, which is Condition 3 in the Emergency Operations Plan.  The highest state of readiness is known as Condition 1
  • Members should plan to stand duty as scheduled.  If you anticipate that you can't make your duty, call EMS-5 at 635-7695.
  • Extra personnel are requested for the before and during the possible strike period.
  • Personnel reporting to the station should bring provisions to sleep on the floor and to stay up to 36 hours if necessary.
  • Off-duty members should prepare their homes and businesses for the storm, and should get ready to evacuate themselves and their families.

For reference, see the other pre-defined states of readiness:

  • Condition 4 (Normal Operations)
  • Condition 2 (Mobilization Alert)
  • Condition 1 (DISASTER ALERT)
