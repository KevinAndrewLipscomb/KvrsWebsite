STANDING RULE
"Proxy Rule"

Date of original adoption:  7 July 1997
Date of latest revision:  None

  1. This rule shall constitute a Standing Rule known as the "Proxy Rule".
  2. The Corporation shall allow proxies subject to the following limitations:
    1. No proxy shall be honored unless it is made in writing as part of a written request for an excused absence, and such request has been granted in accordance with the bylaws.
    2. A member may act as the proxy for at most three absent members.
