VIRGINIA BEACH
DEPARTMENT OF EMERGENCY MEDICAL SERVICES
DUTY FIELD COMMANDER TEAM

Uniform Requirements

  1. Uniforms will be in accordance with the Rules, Regulations and Policies Manual, section 2, #9 for Brigade Commanders.
  2. Members of the Department of Emergency Medical Services riding as observers shall be in the same uniform as the Duty Field Commander minus the oak leaf collar devices on his/her station uniform.
  3. The optional golf shirt is an acceptable uniform for the Duty Chief.
