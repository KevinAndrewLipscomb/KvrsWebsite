Yahoo! Groups

 Add or view comments.

The Yahoo! Groups are freely available only to members of the volunteer rescue squads of Virginia Beach.  The software will not accept posts from nonsubscribers.  It will only release the distribution list to current subscribers.

Don't be afraid of logging in -- you only need to do it the first time (to subscribe), or when you want to browse or search the archives.  Once you subscribe, you don't need your password to receive or send messages.
