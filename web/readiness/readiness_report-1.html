Readiness Report:  DISASTER ALERT (Condition 1)

This is the official KVRS Readiness Report.  Before and during potential states of emergency, check this message frequently for updates.

  • Virginia Beach is currently under a Hurricane Warning.
  • The Department of EMS has issued a Disaster Alert, which is Condition 1, the highest state of readiness, in the Emergency Operations Plan.
  • All available personnel should report to the station to receive their assignments.
  • Bring provisions to sleep on the floor and to stay up to 72 hours if necessary.

For reference, see the other pre-defined states of readiness:

  • Condition 4 (Normal Operations)
  • Condition 3 (Preparedness Alert)
  • Condition 2 (Mobilization Alert)
