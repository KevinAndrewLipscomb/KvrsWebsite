The following membership classes are available for review.  Requests for contact information should be directed to the President.

Charter Members
The founding fathers of the Kempsville Volunteer Fire Department, Incorporated on July 30, 1951.
Life Members
Those who have served at least ten years with the Kempsville Volunteer Rescue Squad or the Kempsville Volunteer Fire Department, or both.
Honorary Members
Those who make special ongoing contributions to KVRS.
Members In Memorium
Those who have died while on the roster of the Kempsville Volunteer Rescue Squad or the Kempsville Volunteer Fire Department, or both.
