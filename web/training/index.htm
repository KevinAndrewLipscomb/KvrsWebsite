Attendant-in-Charge Challenge and blank answer sheet form
We used to give this as an open-book test at the end of a new member's ambulance training period. Nowadays, any AIC may use it to measure their familiarity with the policies and procedures that are specific to our squad and department.  Per departmental training standards, this is no longer part of the AIC release process.
Currents in Emergency Cardiovascular Care
Online quarterly newsletter in which updates to AHA ECCP curricula such as BCLS, ACLS, and PALS are published.  Excellent resource for those who want to know exactly what has changed since the last time they took the course.
