Admin member Dennis Stewart fills oxygen bottles (April 1997). More and more, KVRS depends on "admin members" (those who do not run rescue calls) to keep the squad running. Our admin members handle many of the day-to-day tasks that our patient care providers can not find the time for, like picking up and routing the mail, maintaining our databases, processing donation checks, stocking the ambulances, recovering equipment from hospitals, shuttling vehicles to and from the shop, and maintaining our living spaces. Admin members must fulfill the requirements of their billet. They are encouraged to join the Support Team and to participate in various committees.
